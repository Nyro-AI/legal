Privacy Policy

Nyro · Effective Date: June 2, 2026 · Last Updated: June 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Nyro ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your business data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, store, and protect information when you use the Nyro mobile applications (including Nyro Facturación), web dashboard (app.nyropr.com), or related services (collectively, "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Password (hashed — we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Workspace identifier
  • Authentication tokens (stored in iOS Keychain / Android Keystore on your device)
  • MFA/TOTP enrollment status (we do not store TOTP secrets after setup)

2.2 Invoicing & Expense Data (Nyro Facturación)

When you use Nyro Facturación, we store the business records you create:

  • Products, expenses ("gastos"), amounts, payment method and beneficiary/dealer information
  • Approval and payment status, payment timestamps
  • Images you upload as supporting evidence (dealer wire-info images and payment receipts/"comprobantes")

2.3 Business Data (via Odoo ERP)

If your workspace connects an Odoo ERP instance (e.g. for inventory recommendations), Nyro reads sales, inventory, accounts-receivable, operations, and CRM data in real time. Nyro does not permanently store your Odoo business data — it is fetched per request and used transiently for processing, except for explicitly saved AI briefings.

2.4 Marketing Platform Data (OAuth Integrations)

If you connect marketing or commerce platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, Stripe Connect, Shopify, Mercado Libre, Clover, Mailchimp, Klaviyo), we access their insights/metrics. OAuth access tokens are encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption (AES-128-CBC) and stored in Supabase.

2.5 Device and Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system (iOS/Android)
  • App version
  • Push notification token (FCM) if you opt in to notifications
  • IP address (used for rate limiting and security monitoring)
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Sentry — see Section 4)

2.6 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment card numbers (handled by Stripe — we do not see raw card data)
  • Government ID or social security numbers
  • Health or biometric data
  • Precise GPS location
  • Microphone data or contact-book data

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Locations

  • Supabase (database & storage): Account data, invoicing records, uploaded images, and encrypted OAuth tokens are stored in Supabase-managed PostgreSQL and Storage, hosted on AWS.
  • Device (iOS/Android): Session tokens (JWT) are stored in iOS Keychain or Android Keystore — not in plaintext.
  • Backend (in-memory only): Certain AI responses are cached in memory for up to 30–60 minutes to reduce API costs, then discarded.

5.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS in transit
  • AES encryption for stored OAuth tokens
  • JWT authentication with JWKS verification
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) on the database
  • MFA/TOTP support
  • Input sanitization and rate limiting

7. Cookies and Local Storage

The Nyro mobile applications do not use browser cookies. They use iOS Keychain / Android Keystore for secure session tokens, encrypted shared preferences for non-sensitive settings, and in-memory cache for transient results. The web dashboard (app.nyropr.com) may use session cookies for authentication and local storage for UI preferences.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We share data only: with the service providers listed in Section 4 under data processing agreements; with connected platforms you explicitly authorize via OAuth; with law enforcement if legally required or to prevent imminent harm; and in a business transfer subject to equivalent privacy protections.

Right of access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), portability, objection, restriction of processing, and withdrawal of consent.

9.2 CCPA/CPRA (California residents)

10. Business Data — Special Considerations

Nyro processes commercially sensitive business data (sales, inventory, financials, invoicing). We treat this data as confidential and will not use it for any purpose other than providing the Services to you. If your data includes employee or customer PII, you are responsible for ensuring your use of Nyro complies with your own obligations under applicable law.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored in cloud infrastructure operated by Supabase (AWS) and may be processed by Anthropic's API infrastructure, which may involve transfers outside your country of residence. Where required, such transfers are governed by appropriate safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

12. Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for business owners aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 and will delete such data promptly if discovered.
